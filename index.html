My Latest Projects

Take a look at my recent work.

GNN-RL Stock Predictor

Created a graph network of various stocks where each node contained information about price, RSI, SMA, and MACD and edges were determined by covariance. Using a GNN Autoencoder to reduce the dimensionality of the graph, and RL agent used the encoded data to learn how to trade.

Financial Sentiment Analysis

This project is designed to train 4 different models (Logistic Regression, Multi-layer Perceptron Algorithm, Multi-layer Perceptron Network, Recurrent Neural Network) for sentiment analysis on financial statements. The motivation behind this project is to see if financial news sentiment could be a viable trading strategy. After training each model, they were backtested against the S&P 500 using Positive, Negative, and Neutral predictions on news articles as trading signals.

Energy Forecasting

A Random Forest Regression model that forecasts price movements for UGA and USO (Natural Gas and Crude Oil) based on a custom dataset. The dataset comprised the weekly EIA reports, current presidential approval index, days until the next election, the Fed Funds Rate, as well as the 10yr Treasury rate.
